LINKTYPE_MTP2

Description

Packets are Signaling System No. 7 Message Transfer Part Level 2 (MTP2) packets, as specified by section 2.2 "Signal unit format" of ITU-T Recommendation Q.703 (note that the fields in Figure 3 are transmitted in right-to-left order). The packet does not include the check bits. The packet is not bit-stuffed, so it includes no flag fields.
